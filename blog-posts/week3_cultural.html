How Do I Think?

By Shreya Patel

July 3, 2014

According to the personal thinking style quiz, my thinking style is Abstract Random. I had no idea what that meant, so I did a little research and this is what I found out. As an abstract random thinker, I organize information through reflection, and thrive in unstructured, people-orientated environments.

My mind is always reflecting on my actions, feelings, and ideas. This process brings me to a conclusion that helps in making future decisions, and creating and breaking habits. Relationships are also very important to me, whether they be in personal or professional environments. These people-orientated environments constantly encourage me to be mindful, interactive, and, patient. Although, I think of myself to be free-spirited behavior wise, when it comes to learning I think I thrive in structured environments. I believe this is because, without structure I tend to procrastinate or fall off track.
