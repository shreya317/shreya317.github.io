Margin vs. Border vs. Padding

By Shreya Patel

July 27th, 2014

  • The space outside the blue border is considered the margin.
    • - The margin area is transparent and clears the area around the border.
  • The space between the margin and padding is considered the border.
    • - The border goes around the padding and the content.
  • The space between the border and the content is considered the padding.
    • - The padding clears an area around the content.
  • The content is where your text and images go.

You can adjust the margin, padding, and border as you please
